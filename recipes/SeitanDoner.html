Seitan Döner

Delicious Seitan Döner in just a few simple steps, this recipe manages to stay healthy and delicious at the same time, with the power of Seitan and Chickpeas!

You'll need the following ingredients to follow along:

Ingredients

  • One can Chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 140g Wheat Gluten
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 80g flour
  • 10g Salt
  • 125ml Water
  • Kofte Seasoning

Steps

  1. Rinse the chickpeas under warm water to make the kneading process easier
  2. Drain the water, move chickpeas into a large bowl
  3. Drizzle with olive oil generously
  4. Knead roughly with hands, try applying pressure with your whole body instead of just your arms.
  5. After the chickpeas become a paste, proceed with adding in the dry ingredients.
  6. Mix thoroughly
  7. Add in the water, start folding the seitan in thoughtful patterns, once the gluten gets activated, the form will be hard to change
  8. Try to form the seitan into a potato like shape
  9. Bring about 2L water to a boil in a large pot, make sure the pot is large enough to hold both the water and the seitan
  10. Add broth powder, slowly place the seitan in the water, let simmer for 30 minutes
  11. Slice the seitan into doner like pieces and fry in a pan lightly covered in olive oil until crispy
  12. Enjoy!
